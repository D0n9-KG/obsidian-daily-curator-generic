# Quality checklist

Use this checklist before completing a daily curation run.

## Safety

- Raw notes were not overwritten.
- Existing notes were updated only when clearly matching the same concept/source.
- No bulk move/rename/delete was performed without approval.
- Uncertain classifications went to the unresolved queue.

## Knowledge cards

- One card has one main concept or claim cluster.
- The title is a retrieval handle, not a vague sentence.
- The card has a source backlink.
- The card explains the idea in the user's own words when possible.
- The card links to a related card, source, or MOC/map when possible.

## Sources

- Saved-but-undigested sources are marked to process.
- Digested sources link to derived knowledge cards.
- Time-sensitive factual claims are verified or marked unverified.

## Daily summary

- The summary is useful without rereading the raw daily note.
- The change log says what went where.
- Tasks and open questions are explicit.
- The next quick-capture note is prepared if enabled.
